Hailing from the enchanting landscapes of Nepal, my journey into the realm of computer engineering was nothing short of a captivating adventure. This is the narrative of my passage from the serene foothills of the Himalayas to the bustling campuses of the United States of America—a journey fueled by unrelenting passion and a relentless pursuit of knowledge.

The Early Chapters

My academic journey embarked in Nepal, where I traversed the corridors of high school. Unlike many of my peers who were already engrossed in the world of programming and coding, my introduction to these captivating domains was yet to unfold. Nonetheless, an undeniable curiosity for engineering gripped my heart, propelling me forward.

Dreaming Beyond Borders

The dream of pursuing higher education in the United States began to take shape, but there was a daunting hurdle in my path—my family's financial constraints. The idea of studying abroad seemed like a distant dream, but I was not one to easily concede.

The Beacon of Hope

Determined to transform my dream into a reality, I embarked on an exhaustive quest for opportunities. Through meticulous research and unwavering hope, I curated a list of universities that extended full-ride scholarships to international students—a beacon of hope that would soon illuminate my path.

Bethune-Cookman University: A Turning Point

Amidst the list, Bethune-Cookman University stood as a prominent figure. With my heart set on pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering, I submitted my application and held my breath. The moment I had eagerly awaited arrived—confirmation of a full-ride scholarship at Bethune-Cookman University.

Embarking on a New Chapter

My journey as a Computer Engineering major at Bethune-Cookman University commenced—a new chapter in a foreign land filled with both anticipation and challenges. The world of coding and hardware awaited, and I eagerly embraced the opportunities and knowledge that lay ahead.

In Conclusion

My voyage from the Himalayan valleys of Nepal to the shores of the United States, from a lack of programming background to pursuing a major in Computer Engineering, is a testament to the unwavering power of determination and the pursuit of one's true passion. As I continue to learn, grow, and contribute in the field of technology, I look back on my remarkable journey with profound gratitude for the opportunities that have shaped my path.

If you yearn to delve deeper into the details of my journey, to explore the challenges I faced and the triumphs I celebrated, please do not hesitate to reach out. I am always eager to share my experiences and insights, hoping to inspire and guide those who dare to dream.
